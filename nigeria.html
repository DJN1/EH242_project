Nigerian Creation Myth

The Origin of Death

In the beginning there is neither disease nor death. Everyone lived in a happy world. However, one day a man got sick and died so the people sent a worm to the sky to ask what they should do with the body. The worm started its journey. When he arrived, the sky told him to tell the people that they should hang the body in a fork and throw mush at it until it comes back to life. Doing this would eliminate death and disease for everyone. The worm started his journey back but was very slow. A mischievous lizard heard of this and ran ahead because he wanted to deceive the people. He told the people that he was sent because he was faster and that they were supposed to bury the body in the ground.
